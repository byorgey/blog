About me

I’m Brent Yorgey. I am an associate professor in the department of mathematics and computer science at Hendrix College in Conway, Arkansas, USA. My academic/professional interests include functional programming languages (especially Haskell), (embedded) domain-specific languages, type systems, category theory, combinatorics, discrete mathematics in general, and computer science and mathematics education. Other interests include music (I am a classical and jazz pianist), Go, bridge, and classical Greek and Hebrew.
